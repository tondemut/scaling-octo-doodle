Skin Deep Signature Facial €60

A skin cleansing treatment like no other. Skin is thoroughly cleansed and exfoliated to reveal fresh clear skin, followed by a mask application specifically suited to your skin type. Finish with a totally relaxing face, neck and shoulder massage. Book

Aromatis Facial €75

A customised treatment offering solutions to four common skin problems:

  • Moisturising Aromatis - A powerful moisturising treatment for dry, thirsty skin. Sodium hyaluronate and moisturising ingredients saturate the skin with moisture deep down. Fine lines and wrinkles are smoothed leaving the skin velvety soft.
  • Oxygenating Aromatis - A powerful detoxifying treatment ideal for dull complexions, smokers and skin starved of oxygen. Hydrolysed soy protein activates cell respiration, promotes tissue oxygen and brightens the complexion
  • Firming Aromatis - A firming treatment with tensor effect. Plant-base firming complex (Raffermin + ginseng extract) tightens and firms the face, neck and décolleté.
  • Regenerating Aromatis - A treatment for devitalized, tired skin. Peptide extract improves the skin’s resistance leaving it firmer and better toned. Papaya extract promotes tissue repair and increases skin density

    • Treatment Time: 1 hr

Reponse Vitalite €65

Fatigue, stress, climate changes, pollution...day after day your skin weakens and your complexion loses its radiance. This treatment draws its overall revitalizing action from a complex of vitamins- vitamin A regenerates cellular renewal, viamin C stimulates the skins energy and radiance while vitamin E protects the cells from free radicals. Your skin is recharged with vital energy, your cells regenerate and your skin regains its balance and natural protective abilities.

Treatment Time: 1hr
